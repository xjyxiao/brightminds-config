Privacy Policy

Bright Minds · Last updated: June 17, 2026

Summary

Bright Minds is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information. Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

Information we do not collect

The app does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email, phone number, contacts, location, photos, or any other personal data. We do not use analytics, advertising, or third‑party tracking SDKs.

Data stored on your device

Your game progress, scores, streaks, preferences (such as appearance and sound settings), and your answers to the optional well‑being check‑ins are saved only on your device using the system's local storage. This information is never transmitted to us or anyone else. Deleting the app removes this data.

Network use

The app may occasionally download a small configuration file from our public GitHub repository to enable or disable features. This is a one‑way, read‑only request and contains no personal information about you.

Well‑being check‑ins

The app includes optional, light self‑reflection check‑ins (for example about mood or how content you feel). These are provided for personal awareness and relaxation only. They are processed on your device, are not a medical service, and do not provide diagnosis or treatment. If you have health concerns, please consult a qualified professional.

Children's privacy

Bright Minds is suitable for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app collects no personal data at all, no such information is gathered from users of any age.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at brightminds.support@gmail.com.
